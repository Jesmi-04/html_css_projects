BUDGET 2022-23

Prime Minister NARENDRA MODI'S government laid out
India’s budget for the financial year starting April 1,
months after a devastating second Covid-19 wave that
crippled small businesses and deepened the country’s
already vast inequalities.

Finance minister NIRMALA SITHARAMAN on February 1,2022(Tuesday) presented a budget that calls for a bigger spend to fire up growth in Asia’s third-largest economy,as it stages a world-beating recovery from the pandemic. Sitharaman proposed increasing the size of the economy’s annual spending to 39.5 trillion rupees ($529 billion) to support growth plans.
